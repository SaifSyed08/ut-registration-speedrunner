Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 1, 2026

UT Registration SpeedRunner ("the Extension") is designed with privacy in mind. The Extension does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information.

Information Stored

The Extension stores only the information you choose to enter, including:

  • Course names
  • Course Unique Numbers
  • Extension settings and preferences

This information is stored locally on your device using Chrome's Storage API. It never leaves your browser and is never transmitted to external servers.

How Information Is Used

The locally stored information is used solely to provide the Extension's functionality, including:

  • Saving course queues and backup sections
  • Remembering your settings between browser sessions
  • Allowing keyboard shortcuts to quickly insert queued Unique Numbers
  • Displaying the current queue position during registration

Data Sharing

The Extension does not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Transmit data to external servers
  • Sell or share user data with third parties
  • Use analytics or tracking technologies
  • Display advertisements

Permissions

The Extension requests only the permissions necessary to:

  • Store your course queues and settings locally.
  • Access supported UT registration pages and the project mock practice page to display the overlay and insert queued Unique Numbers when you use the Extension's keyboard shortcuts.
  • Temporarily check the active tab when you open the popup so the Extension can tell whether it is available on the current page.

The Extension does not access unrelated websites. Its supported pages are UT registration-related pages and the project mock practice page.

Changes to This Policy

If the Extension's privacy practices change in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated before those changes take effect.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the project's GitHub repository.
