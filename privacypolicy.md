# Privacy Policy for UT Registration SpeedRunner

**Effective Date:** July 1, 2026

UT Registration SpeedRunner is designed with privacy in mind. The extension does **not collect, transmit, sell, or share** any personal information.

## Information We Collect

The extension does **not** collect or transmit any user data.

The only information stored is the course Unique Numbers, course names, and user preferences that you choose to save while using the extension. This information is stored **locally on your device** using Chrome's storage API.

## How Your Data Is Used

Stored data is used solely to provide the extension's functionality, including:

* Saving your course queues and backup Unique Numbers.
* Remembering your extension settings and preferences.
* Displaying your current queue position during registration.

## Data Sharing

UT Registration SpeedRunner does **not**:

* Send data to external servers.
* Share data with third parties.
* Sell user data.
* Use analytics or tracking technologies.
* Display advertisements.

## Data Security

Because all extension data is stored locally in your browser, it remains under your control. Removing the extension or clearing its stored data will delete this information.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

If the extension's data practices change in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated before those changes take effect.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the Chrome Web Store listing or the project's GitHub repository.
